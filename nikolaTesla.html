The Life of Nikola Tesla:

  • 1856: Nikola Tesla was born on July 10 in the Austrian Empire (modern-day Croatia).

  • 1861: Tesla attended primary school. Tesla was able to do Calculus in his head, which lead teachers to believe he was cheating.

  • 1873: Graduating, Tesla finishes a four year school term in 3 years. Shortly after, Tesla contracts cholera and is nearly killed by it.

  • 1882: Offered a job working with the Continental Edison Company in Paris.
